About Us

SINO was incorporated in Hong Kong in 2000, primarily as a trader of apparel accessories. In 2007, the company acquired a fully-fledged metal trims manufacturing plant in China to address the challenges encountered with third-party metal injection technologies.

Today, the company supplies a wide range of garment accessories, from metal to plastic and eco-friendly trims.

Our team comprises of sales and customer service personnel deployed in China, India, Sri Lanka, and Bangladesh. They work closely with the manufacturing plant and sourcing centre in China to execute customer inquiries and orders.

The entire process is managed by the executive leadership at the head office in Hong Kong.
