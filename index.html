About Us

Sino Terry was incorporated in Hong Kong in 2000 as a manufacturer and trader of apparel acessories made of metal, plastic and recycled materials. All our products adhere to US and EU quality standards.

Fully owned alloy injection plant is situated in Guandong China, with other partnerted factories situated in and around the mainland.

The Sino team comprises of sales and customer service personnel deployed in China, India, Sri Lanka, and Bangladesh. They work closely with the manufacturing plant and sourcing centre in China to execute customer inquiries and orders.

The entire process is managed by the executive leadership at the head office in Hong Kong.
